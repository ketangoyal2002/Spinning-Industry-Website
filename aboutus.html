About Us

Shree Hari Spintex Ltd. is a certified company from BCI & Gots.

M/S SHREE HARI SPINTEX LTD is a cotton spinning manufecturing unit equipped with latest state art of technology m/cs, situated at MAUR MANDI, Dist Bathinda PUNJAB Since April 2007 on Bathinda Patiala national highway about 30 km away from Bathinda. Company is formatted by a group of businessmen, who already have their business in cotton Ginning, Rice sellers and oil mills.

Shree Hari Spintex Ltd has its own cotton ginning unit and is equipped with latest state art of technology m/c right from Ginning, Pressing to Spinning with all type of latest utilities

Spinning m/cs right from Blow room to Ring Frame are from India’s best spinning m/cs manufacturer M/S Lakshmi Machine Works Ltd Coimbatore, TFO Savio India Ltd. and winding m/c Autoconer AC-5 and AC- X5 are from world’s best manufacturer M/S Saurer textile solutions Pvt. Ltd Germany previously known as Schlaforst Germany ,equipped with USTER QUANTUM –II Yarn clearers with online informator.

Shree Hari is producing best quality cotton yarn such as CARDED, COMBED, SLUB, TFO (DOUBLE) and COMPACT YARN for weaving, terry towel and Knitting. company has its own all required utilities such as waste recovery system from m/s Batliboi Ltd, Compressor from ATLAS COPCO, yarn conditioning from Sieger.Company Laboratoy equipped with latest Testing Instrument from PREMIER to check the quality at stages of yarn manufacturing right from the Fiber stages.

Although the Plant is running on POWER through independent feeder from PSEB still company have its own stand by 1000KVA self power Generator with 500KVA each two DG sets from m/s Sudhir Gensets.

Company has sufficient qualified professional staff and skilled manpower having vast experience in cotton spinning.

Company has its own quality control system and worker training facilities.Shree Hari Spintex Ltd is committed to fulfill the customer’s requirements in time with entire satisfaction.

Mission & Vision

Shree Hari Spintex was started in 2007 with a mission to be a leader in the cotton yarn exports from India. Vision of the company is to be the one shop for all kinds of yarns for the customers and provide complete solution with highest quality to best customer service experience.
